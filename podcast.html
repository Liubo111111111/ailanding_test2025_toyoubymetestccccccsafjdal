A weekly podcast exploring how AI is transforming creation, business, and human potential. I talk with builders, creators, and thinkers about what's actually working right now.

Each episode focuses on practical applications of AI that you can use today. No hype, no theoretical future-speak - just real tools and techniques from people building the future.

The podcast started as a way to document my own journey from content creator to product builder. What began as personal experiments turned into a movement of creators discovering they could build more than they ever thought possible.

I release new episodes every Tuesday. Each one includes the exact prompts, tools, and workflows I used to build something real that week.
