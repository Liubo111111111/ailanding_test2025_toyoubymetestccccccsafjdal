Apps & Products

Real applications I've built entirely with AI assistance. No traditional coding required.

YapThread

A transcription app that converts voice memos into organized, searchable threads. Built using Claude and ChatGPT, currently earning $12,000/month.

What started as a personal tool to transcribe my own voice notes became a product used by thousands of creators, journalists, and professionals who need to quickly capture and organize their thoughts.

VibeCode

The mobile app builder I co-founded. While not built with AI, it's the platform that enables AI app creation for thousands of others.

Clone Challenges

I've live-streamed building functional clones of major applications:

  • Clubhouse clone - 60 minutes, fully functional audio rooms
  • Notion clone - 4.5 hours, real-time collaborative documents
  • Twitter clone - 90 minutes, posting, feeds, and profiles
  • TikTok clone - 2 hours, video upload, feed, and interactions
  • Uber clone - 3 hours, maps, ride requests, and payments

The Process

Each app follows the same workflow: start with a clear prompt, iterate with AI, test on device, refine based on user feedback. The key is treating AI as a collaborative partner rather than just a code generator.

These aren't just demos - they're real apps that people use daily. The transcription app alone processes thousands of hours of audio every month.

The lesson: when you remove the barrier of traditional programming, creators can focus on solving real problems instead of wrestling with technical implementation.
